About me

I am a final-year undergraduate student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), the leading engineering institution in Bangladesh. Prior to this, I completed my SSC and HSC at Mirzapur Cadet College, a prestigious Army-affiliated institution known for its excellence in academics and discipline.

I am currently pursuing my undergraduate thesis in the Graph Drawing and Information Visualization Lab, under the mentorship of Professor Dr. Md. Saidur Rahman. My research focuses on Graph Theory, specifically addressing the K-dispersion problem on a Circle. I am working toward developing a linear-time solution for this problem, building upon the existing solution for the K-dispersion problem on a Line, introduced by Professor Shinichi Nakano.

I also have a keen interest in Machine Learning, and I am currently working on a project to develop a Deep Learning Model for predicting rainfall in Bangladesh. For this, I have compiled a time-series dataset, having weather data from 1946 to 2022, collected from various weather stations across the country.

During my undergraduate studies, I have contributed to several full-stack development projects as part of my coursework. These group projects have not only enhanced my technical skills but also strengthened my ability to work effectively in team environments.

Beyond academics, I am an avid athlete and have been a midfielder of the BUET Football Team since my first year. In 2022, I was awarded with the Player of the Tournament in the Inter Hall Football Tournament, as a recognition of my performance on the field.

In my free time, I like to enjoy with my family. I am also a big fan of travelling and watching movies.
